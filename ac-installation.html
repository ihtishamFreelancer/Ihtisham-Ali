Installing a cooling system or heating system correctly is of utmost importance. If it is improperly connected, your system will provide insufficient cooling/heating and may not operate efficiently; it can also create safety and health hazards.

A/R Cooling Center offers quality services at affordable prices with a commitment to customer satisfaction. Our team of experienced engineers is equipped with the latest technology and tools to handle any project installation task.

We can install different types of AC/HVAC or Heating Systems and their components, along with thermostats, and even address areas of your home that need to be insulated. We install leading brands of equipment and provide comprehensive maintenance plans and warranties.

We offer complete services for:

  • Air Conditioning Units Installation
  • HVAC System Installation
  • Heating System Installation
