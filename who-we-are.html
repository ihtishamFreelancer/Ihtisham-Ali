Who We Are

A/R Cooling Center is one of the leading and professional service providers in the field of Services, Operation and Maintenance of Heating, Ventilation, Air Conditioning (HVAC) and Refrigeration facilities. Established in 2011, A/R Service Center has continually set high standards by providing exemplary professional expertise to improve the efficiency and performance of HVAC and Air Conditioning Plants.

Our engineering and supervisory staff includes qualified personnel, technicians, and skilled operators with years of experience and is capable of responding to the demands of modern industries regarding HVAC and Air Conditioning. Our team is well-versed in the safety requirements of various industries and engineering standards.

We pride ourselves on integrity, honesty, and the highest levels of workmanship by offering various services ranging from minor residential repairs to large commercial project installations.

We are putting into action business for coexistence with our commitment to fulfilling our social roles and responsibilities, and we are putting all our efforts to satisfy customers’ specific needs. Our company has focused on offering services by implementing integrity, transparency, and honesty toward our clients. We strive to prove our competence in providing quality services and assistance.

We value our clients, which is why we exceed expectations and are always on time.
